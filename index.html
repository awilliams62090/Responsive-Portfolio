About Me

Hello! Thank you for stopping by my page. As an almost native to Colorado (been here since I was a mere 6 weeks old), I enjoy living in the 4 seasons we have. I left for a few years in 2008-2009 to try my luck in Vegas (didn't work out as I had planned) and then again from 2012-2015 to pursue a career down in Southern Utah at Lake Powell. I have to say I have had some great adventures in my early twenties and am excited to see what my late twenties and beyond brings me! I currently work in the wedding and events industry behind the scenes in administration/human resources. I am always looking to develop my career and expand my network base as well as my own personal brain power, which is what has landed me in the DU Bootcamp program!

When I am not learning, you can find me in the kitchen, crafting new recipes and dazzling people with my amature chef skills. I also enjoy music, movies, enjoying all that Colorado has to offer, and of course, spending as much time as I can with my pup, Malibu. She is my partner in crime and we compliment each other's crazy, sometimes a little too well I think!
